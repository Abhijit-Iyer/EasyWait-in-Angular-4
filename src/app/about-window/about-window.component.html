What is this?

This application majorly focuses on reducing the long queues at the vendor's workplace and provides an easy interface to the vendor for managing his/her queues.

It also focuses on its customers, where they can view queue statuses and pre-book visiting appointments.



If you are a customer..

You have an interface to pre-book appointments remotely from your hood and check the status of appointments.

Using the app, you can manage your departure time from your home; thereby reaching the vendor's place on time.



If you are a vendor..

An application where a vendor can start a queue, remotely from his place and manage it for accepting appointments and registrations for services provided at his workplace.
